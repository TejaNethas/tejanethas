Hey, It's time to know about me!

A strategic and collaborative professional with a demonstrated history of driving innovation and delivering outstanding user experiences. Experienced in building and leading cross-functional design teams, fostering an environment of creativity, collaboration, and excellence. Skilled in aligning design initiatives with business objectives while working closely with stakeholders, executives, and product managers. A persuasive communicator with a deep understanding of UX/UI principles and the entire design process—from concept ideation and prototyping to usability testing and final implementation. Proven ability to effectively present design strategies, concepts, and outcomes to both technical and non-technical audiences, showcasing the value of design and its contribution to business success.

Over 10+ years of experience in UI/UX

I have had the privilege of working across a diverse range of domains, each presenting unique challenges and opportunities for innovation. In the healthcare sector, I contributed to creating user-centered solutions that prioritize patient experience and data accessibility. My work in banking focused on enhancing security and usability in financial applications, ensuring seamless user experiences. In the e-commerce and travel industries, I designed intuitive interfaces to optimize customer journeys, while in charity and transportation, I developed solutions that drive engagement and efficiency. Additionally, I gained valuable experience in GE’s subdomains, including GE Digital, GE Aviation, GE Healthcare, and Fast&Up Nutrition, where I applied cutting-edge technologies to create impactful digital experiences across various industries
